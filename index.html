#11 Short Squeeze Setup

Hedge funds are heavily short while institutions are not bearish — a dangerous divergence for the shorts. The combined positioning looks bearish because leveraged money is dragging it down, but the "smart money" (long-only asset managers) is holding or even adding. This creates high squeeze potential: any positive catalyst can trigger a violent short-covering rally as leveraged funds are forced to buy back positions. This is a contrarian long signal, especially when combined with improving macro data or a dovish policy shift.

Bias ↑ the options market is leaning bullish — more call activity / positive dealer gamma. Bias ↓ bearish lean — put-heavy positioning or negative gamma. Bias → no clear directional tilt. Adv. rec is the engine's overall call combining the bias, skew, and move size — read it like a technicals rating (STRONG_BUY strongest conviction up, STRONG_SELL strongest down, HOLD = no edge). Confidence (●●● High → ○○○ Very Low) reflects how much data backed the read — a Low-confidence STRONG_BUY is a weak signal. Gamma target is the price the dealer book magnetically pulls toward: call_wall = a ceiling of dealer resistance above spot, put_wall = a floor of dealer support below spot, zero_gamma = the level where dealer hedging flips from dampening moves to amplifying them.

Bull-coil the stock is compressing with an upward lean — energy is building for a potential breakout higher. Bear-coil same compression but pointing down — potential breakdown. Neither means the move has started; it means the conditions are in place. sqz (squeeze) Bollinger Bands have tightened inside the Keltner Channel — volatility is at a low ebb and a sharp move (either direction) is historically likely soon. dry (volume dry-up) trading volume has contracted sharply — participation is falling away, which often precedes a breakout as the market decides a direction. +div (pending bull divergence) price has been making lower lows but one or more momentum indicators are making higher lows — the down-move may be running out of steam. The indicator(s) shown in brackets are which of RSI / MACD-Line / MACD-Hist / CMF / OBV is flagging the divergence. RSI and MACD are the most reliable; CMF and OBV add volume confirmation. −div (pending bear divergence) the mirror: price making higher highs but momentum fading — the up-move may be losing conviction; same indicator breakdown applies. ma-tight (MA compression) short- and long-term moving averages are converging — the trend is pausing and a new directional move is setting up. RS vs SPY how the stock's price trend is behaving relative to the S&P 500 — positive means it has been outperforming the index, negative means underperforming.
